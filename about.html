🧭Purpose of the Web App

Plan Ahead was created to simplify course registration and academic planning for students. Instead of juggling Excel sheets, clashing slots, and confusing timetables, this platform brings everything into one clean, student-friendly interface.

The idea came directly from student struggles — missed courses due to slot confusion, stress during registration week, and no easy way to visualize your week. This tool eliminates those pain points.

👥Who It's For

This platform is tailored for the real needs of:

  • Students who want to register quickly, avoid slot clashes, and manage their academic plans visually.
  • Admins who need to upload and manage course data, slot timings, and simplify backend handling.

Whether you're in your first semester or final year, Plan Ahead helps you make informed decisions during course selection.

🏗️Key Features

Every feature in Plan Ahead exists because students like you asked for it, faced issues with older systems, or needed more clarity.

  • 📌 Conflict-Free Selection: Add only valid courses — no more overlapping slots or double bookings.
  • 🗓️ Live Timetable Preview: Instantly see how your week looks with selected courses filled in automatically.
  • 📤 PDF Export: One-click download of your final course list and weekly timetable for offline use or sharing.
  • 🕒 Auto-Saved Timetables: Keeps your previous course selections locally for 7 days, even if you close the tab.
  • 🎨 Clean UI: No clutter. Just a focused, mobile-friendly design that helps you think clearly and act faster.
  • 🔐 Admin Tools: Upload full course catalogs and slot mappings using Excel or CSV templates.

💡Vision & Future Plans

Plan Ahead is built by a student for students — and that mission drives every next step. The platform will continue to evolve based on real campus needs, feedback, and smarter tools.

  • 📲 Mobile App: Native Android/iOS support for on-the-go course tracking and instant updates.
  • 📅 Future Semester Planning: Add potential future courses and test out what-if scenarios before registration opens.
  • 🔔 Real-Time Notifications: Get notified when a slot time table and course catalog updated for each semester.

Plan Ahead will continue to grow — guided by student input, inspired by real problems, and driven by the idea that smart planning leads to better academic success.
